Some jumbled,  
Some mumbled,  
words made their way  
through the crevices of my mouth.  

But I thought you knew  
my words do not have access  
to what my heart goes through.  

For though my words couldn't dare,  
my eyes did bear,  
the whole truth,  
I solemnly swear.
